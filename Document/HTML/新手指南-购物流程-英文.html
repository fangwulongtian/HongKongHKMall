How to Buy
7 Steps to Start Easy Shopping
1.Register as a member
Click “Register” on the top left corner of webpage;

Follow the prompts to complete the registration process.

2.Log in
Click “Log In” on the top left corner of homepage to log in to Huika Mall Hong Kong.
Look for product
Browse by category or directly search the product.
4.Add to cart
Add the item to shopping cart and then proceed to checkout or continue shopping.
5.Submit order
Select a payment method and then submit the order.
Check order status
Two states “Await shipment” and “Await receipt” are included.
Write a review
Rate and make a comment on the item after receipt.
