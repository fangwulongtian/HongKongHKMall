Ⅰ About Payment
1. Why was the payment unsuccessful?

Probably because:

A. The corresponding bank/Omise/Paypal account doesn't have sufficient balance.

B. An exception occurs to the third party's payment system data transmission.

C. Network problem. Check the network connection, reopen the browser or restart the computer.

Ⅱ About Delivery
1. What should I do if the existing address is unclear or needs changing?

Please directly contact our online service staff and provide the accurate address.

2. When will the product be shipped after successfully ordered?

The product will be shipped within 1~3 working days upon successful payment. Further notice will be given if the order is placed on holidays or festivals, such as National Day, Spring Festival, etc.

Ⅲ About Receipt & After-sale
1. How to sign for delivery and confirm the receipt of product?
Please examine the product in courier’s presence and sign for delivery after no mistake is found, and then confirm the receipt on website; the system will automatically confirm the receipt of product 20 days after shipment by Seller if no receipt confirmation is issued.
Ⅳ About Full Money Back
1. What's Full Money Back?
Full Money Back means that the money you've spent on Huika Mall will be 100% returned in Huimi (a virtual currency on Huika Mall) to your Huika account day by day until all is returned.
2. When will the full money back reward start?
The date of money return for all products will be clearly indicated before your every purchase.
3. How is full money back reward calculated?
Member will get 100% of consumption amount rebates in Huimi for every purchase on Huika Mall. The Huimi rebates a member receives per day during the event will be the order amount (excluding shipping fees) multiplied by 100% and divided by 1,500 (days). Full Money Back reward is calculated for one single order at a time; the amount of rebates per day reserves 2 digits after the decimal point (rounded) and the shortage will be reimbursed on the final rebate day to ensure 100% Huimi reward back to member as promised.【Full Money Back reward starts 7 days upon confirmation of goods received. Huika promises each purchase you make on Huika Mall will earn you 100% Huimi rebates on every dollar you spend but does not commit to the specific end date of full money back to member; the Huimi rebates a member receives per day will be calculated based on 1,500 days during the event.】

Rules for Full Money Back:

Computational formula: Rebates per day (excluding shipping fees) = Total rebates ÷ Rebate days

Start date: Full Money Back reward starts 7 days upon confirmation of goods received.

End date: Full Money Back reward ends upon the amount of rebates up to total rebates.

Method: All the rebates go to your Huika account.

Goods return rule: In case of return failure, you will also receive the Full Money Back reward for the product you want to return.

Ⅴ About Security
1. How to improve the security of my account?
You can increase the account safety by frequently changing password, binding account to email and phone and setting a more complicated payment code in "My Huika - Account Settings - Account Security" after login.
2. Why is my account showed abnormal after login?
If there is something abnormal with your account when logging in, please change the password and contact Customer Service for further questions.
3. What should I do if I forgot my password?
Please click the link "Forgot password" and get into the process of finding login password.
