Return Policy
1. We offer returns and replacements within 7 days from the date of delivery marked by Seller in order and after-sale repairs within 14 days (7 days from seller-marked delivery date + 7 days upon transaction succeeded). You can contact our service personnel for the application of returns, replacements or repairs.
2. Rule to determine seller-marked delivery date:
Order delivered by Huika Mall: Subject to the date of delivery marked in order.
3. For items without any kind of defects or problem, Huika Mall promises:
As long as the item is not used or altered in any way (in the same condition as it was received), we offer full refund (excluding return shopping costs, which shall be born by customer) within 7 days from seller-marked delivery date. Sellers in Huika Mall are selling brand-new products, and for the benefit of customers, the following conditions are considered as items being altered:

3.1. For phones, cameras and laptops: Original packaging has been opened or batteries have been put on for start-up;
3.2. For clothing, shoes and hats: packaging or tag has been damaged, or item has been washed or worn;
3.3. For sealing products: Original packaging has been opened;
3.4. Product has been energized, in water or inserted into slot for use;
3.5. Return of diamonds, golds, watches, jewelry and accessories is not acceptable;
3.6. Return of food, health care products, personal care supplies, intimates, cosmetics, virtual commodities and software is not acceptable;
3.7. Product has clear statement of return not supported at the time of purchase;
3.8. Other conditions that affect the reselling of products.
4. Note: Returns and replacements will not be accepted under the circumstances below:
4.1. Product is not sold in Huika Mall (unmatched Serial No.);
4.2. Product is out of warranty;
4.3. Quality problem is caused by unauthorized maintenance, misuse, collision, negligence, liquid inflow, accident, alteration and incorrect installation, or the tag, machine number and anti-fake label has been torn down or altered;
4.4. Quality problem is caused by unauthorized maintenance, misuse, collision, negligence, liquid inflow, accident, alteration and incorrect installation, or the tag, machine number and anti-fake label has been torn down or altered;
4.5. Product has been used (except quality problem);
4.6. Other conditions that returns and replacements does not apply according to law.
5. Supplement to Rules for Returns and Replacements:
Once payment succeeded, you can contact Customer Service to apply for refund and return within 7 days from the marked delivery date, otherwise, you will not be able to return an item once the deal is closed. If after-sale repairs needed due to quality problem, please apply for after-sale repair service (see Help Center- Return Policy for details) and contact service personnel to settle within 7 days upon close of transaction. Returns and replacements will not be provided 7 days after close of transaction; however, under special circumstance, refund may be promised upon mutual negotiation and the amount of sale price minus intermediate price differentials (namely distributed profits for distributor) will be refunded.
e.g.: You have purchased a product of RMB 100 in Huika Mall (Say the supply price is RMB 80 and intermediate price differential is RMB 20)

5.1 You will receive full refund of RMB 100 after you apply for refund and return within 7 days from the seller-marked delivery date.
5.2 You can apply for after-sale repair service (see Help Center- Return Policy for details) and contact service personnel to settle within 7 days upon close of transaction.
5.3 You will not be able to apply for returns and replacements or after-sale repair service 7 days after close of transaction. However, you can contact service personnel to return or replace an item for special reasons; just be aware of it, the refund amount is RMB 80 (namely supply price) and the intermediate price differentials of RMB 20 cannot be refunded as distributed profits.
