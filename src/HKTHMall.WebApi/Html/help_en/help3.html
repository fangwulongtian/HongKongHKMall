Payment Method
1. How to pay
Use account balance to directly pay the bill. If lack of balance, please top up first and then conduct the payment.
 Pay by visa or Paypal. If there is no sufficient money in visa & Paypal, use account balance to pay.
2. How to top up
Top up by visa or Paypal. Go to top up

Tips:
System prompt of payment succeeded will be given when you finish the payment; if not, you can check the account balance. If the money has been deducted, it means you’ve paid the bill successfully. However, if you fail to pay due to network disconnection or other reasons, you can find the unpaid order in “My Account> Orders” after login to finish your payment.
