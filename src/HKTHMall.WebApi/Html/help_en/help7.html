3. About delivery
Q： What to do when the address is not detailed or needs modification?
A：You can leave a message for online service staff to change the address.

Q： When will the product be shipped after successfully ordered?
A： The product will be shipped within 1~3 working days after successful payment. Further notice will be given if orders placed over the Holidays and Festivals, such as National Day, Spring Festival, etc.
4. About sign-off and after-sale
Q： How to sign off and confirm receipt of goods?
A： Please examine the product in courier’s presence and sign off after no mistake is found, and then confirm receipt of the product in the background; the system will automatically confirm the receipt of goods 20 days after shipment by Seller if no goods received confirmation is issued.
5. About security
Q： How to improve the security of my account?
A： You can increase the account safety by frequently changing password, binding account to email and phone and setting a more complicated payment code in “My Huika- Account Settings- Account Security” after login.

Q： Why the account showed abnormal when I log in?
A： If there is something abnormal with your account when logging in, please change the password and contact Customer Service for further questions.

Q： What to do when I forget my password?
A： Please click the link “Forgot your password” and access to the flow of finding login password.
