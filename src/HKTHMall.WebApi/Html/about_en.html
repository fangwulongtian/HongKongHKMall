Huika International, part of Huika Shiji (HKSJ) Information Technology Co., Ltd., is a prepaid and time-limited shopping platform that HKSJ has been focused on creating.
With registered capital of RMB 50 million, HKSJ is headquartered in Shenzhen, Guangdong, and has nearly 1,000 employees by now, including R & D team of over 500 and operation team of approximately 500. It has set up branches and subsidiaries in Hongkong, Beijing, Shanghai, Suzhou of Jiangsu, Yantai and Longkou of Shandong, Dejiang of Guizhou, Thailand and the United States. Meanwhile the establishment of business divisions in Singapore, Vietnam and other Southeast Asian countries is in progress.
The Company is about to set up operation centers in all major cities across the world, which incorporate enterprise-establishing, procuring, warehousing and training as a whole to support quick selection of quality goods and provide matching services in nationwide scope.
